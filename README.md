# 📱 E-commerce iPhone Resale Market Intelligence (USA)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the refurbished iPhone resale market in the United States using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides insights into pricing trends, sales performance, seller performance, and storage variant demand across major online resale platforms.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

To identify profitable iPhone models, understand pricing patterns, compare resale platforms, and support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Conditional Formatting
- Excel Formulas

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Total Units Sold
- Average Selling Price
- Average Discount %
- Best Selling iPhone Model
- Highest Revenue Platform
- Top Seller
- Most Popular Storage Variant

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- Revenue by iPhone Model
- Revenue by Platform
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Seller Performance
- Storage Variant Analysis
- Battery Health Distribution
- Condition Analysis
- Price Comparison
- Dynamic Filters using Slicers

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- iPhone 13 and iPhone 14 generated the highest revenue.
- 128GB models accounted for the highest sales volume.
- eBay and Back Market contributed the majority of sales.
- Excellent-condition devices sold at higher average prices.
- Certified refurbished devices achieved better customer demand.
